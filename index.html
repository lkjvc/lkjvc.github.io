About Me

Oi, name's Luka, age is 19. Welcome to my very own website. Here, you can find all my work that I'm (un)proud of. I study - or at least try to study - audio & video technologies at the Academy of Technical and Art Applied Studies, School of Electrical and Computer Engineering in Belgrade.

I do lots of stuff. Everything I do is based on my irrationally creative mind. My inspiration comes from utterly random things. The fuel is habitually coffee, and the motivation can be anything: love, travelling, respect, perception of conveying my own ideas, and what not.

Other than what is stated down below, in free time I am likely to try new things or practice old hobbies. I play music on a guitar and Serbian flute, I sing along for myself, I mess around with music in DAWs, I play video games, I watch movies and shows, I drink beer (yes, the taste is really exquisite), and take part in many other shenanigans as long as it blends in nicely with my creativity at some point.
